# Online-Crop-Sales-System

**Project Overview**

The Online Crop Sales System is a web-based application designed to facilitate the online sale and purchase of crops, seeds, and vegetables. The platform connects farmers and customers by providing detailed information about agricultural products and enabling secure online ordering and payment. The system simplifies crop purchasing, reduces time consumption, and offers a user-friendly interface for managing agricultural sales.

**Features**

**Admin Module**
Secure admin login
Manage crop, seed, and vegetable details
View registered users
Monitor customer orders
Track payments and sales
Add, edit, update, and delete records

**Customer Module**
User registration and login
Browse available crops, seeds, and vegetables
View product details and prices
Place orders online
Make secure online payments
Track order information

**Farmer Module**
Farmer registration and login
Add vegetable information
Manage available products
View sales-related information

**Problem Statement**
Traditional crop purchasing methods require farmers and buyers to spend significant time searching for products and market information. Existing systems often lack:

Online ordering facilities
Online payment options
Product knowledge and recommendations
Fixed pricing
Efficient product search mechanisms

**Proposed Solution**

The Online Crop Sales System provides:

Digital marketplace for agricultural products
Easy product search and recommendations
Online ordering and payment
Secure and user-friendly interface
Centralized database management
Efficient communication between farmers and customers
